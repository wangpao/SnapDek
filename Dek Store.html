# 卡片内容

1. I often listen to music while commuting because it helps me relax and pass the time.

我通勤时常听音乐，因为这能让我放松，也打发时间。

2. Drinking a glass of warm water in the morning has become part of my daily routine.

每天早上喝一杯温水已经成了我的日常习惯。

3. On weekends, I usually clean my apartment and do some grocery shopping for the week.

周末我通常会打扫公寓，并采购下一周的食物。

4. I always try to go to bed before midnight to make sure I get enough sleep.

我总是尽量在午夜前睡觉，以确保有充足的睡眠。
